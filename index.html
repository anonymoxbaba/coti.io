Sensitive data transmitted as public information on a blockchain is limiting blockchain usage and adoption.

COTI V2 solves this by enabling end-to-end encryption across Web3 applications. As an EVM-compatible L2, COTI V2 provides a developer-friendly ecosystem that has fast and scalable access to Ethereum.

A Breakthrough in Privacy:
Garbled Circuits

Lighter

The design simplicity of garbled circuits results in an encryption that requires up to 250 times lighter computation and storage than other privacy solutions. This means COTI V2 can operate on any device!

Faster

In addition to the speed and scale of an L2, the lower latency of garbled circuit computation means COTI V2 can settle transactions up to ten times faster than existing smart contract privacy solutions.

Private

Garbling protocols offer a revolutionary approach to handling transactional privacy for multiple parties while still allowing network computation. The contents of a transaction are encrypted so that only the involved parties can see the full details.

A Whole New World of Use Cases

DEXs

Private transactions give users on decentralized finance applications the features they love on CEX, like proprietary trading and protection against slippage losses from MEV exploits.

Privacy Wallets

Private payments, confidential transaction history and real-world asset tokenization.

Governance

Confidential on-chain governance will help to reduce voter coercion, improve electoral integrity as well as preserve the privacy of DAO members.
